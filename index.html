— About me —

I feel lucky to have studied what I wanted, but many people are not that lucky. My dream now is to help to make a world where no matters wherever the people come or how much money their parents are making, all can have great careers, dreams and the same success possibilities based on their skills. I believe that the education is the key to reach this.

I grew up in Concepción, Chile, living just with my mother. She would retire when I supposed to start the college and I knew that with her salary we could not pay any university fee, then, I realized what I had to do if I wanted my dreams come truth: be good enough to get a full scholarship. I studied for my own and I had the best university selection test score of mi high school promotion, many universities asked me to join them for free and I could even choose any national university to study.

While I was in the Industrial Engineering program I worked in many jobs (from cleaning assistant to rental shop fitter in a ski resort abroad) to pay my personal expenses, but I managed to keep being good enough in college to get another scholarship to study a master (at the same time that the other program) in a field that I had fall in love: Computer Science. Here my main area of study has been Data Science.

To come my dream truth, first I would like to work in a software company solving great challenges, because the bigger the challenge, the more amazing people I will meet (I can’t change the world alone) and the more I will learn.

Do you have an awesome project? Contact me! I will be happy to hear about it.
